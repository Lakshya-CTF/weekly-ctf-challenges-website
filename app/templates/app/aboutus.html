What is Capture the Flag?

A capture the flag (CTF) contest is a special kind of cybersecurity competition designed to challenge its participants to solve computer security problems.

The game consists of a series of challenges where participants must reverse engineer, break, hack, decrypt, or do whatever it takes to solve the challenge. The challenges are all set up with the intent of being hacked, making it an excellent, legal way to get hands-on experience.

What is Lakshya CTF?

Lakshya is a capture the flag event at Impetus & Concepts, where you are given challenges based on:

  1. Linux/ Unix commands
  2. Cryptography
  3. Web exploitation
  4. Steganography
  5. Computer networking
  6. Reverse engineering
  7. Pwning
  8. Digital Forensics

Why Lakshya?

The aim of Lakshya CTF is to help students learn about cyber security by giving them hands on experience. It is a gateway to prepare students to face the Information Security world. All you need is some presence of mind and Googling skills to get through!
